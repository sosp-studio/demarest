Luke Demarest

Luke Demarest is an artist, researcher, and educator whose practice explores emergence, simulation, and computational pattern-making. He is the founder and director of sosp.studio and currently teaches at Goldsmiths. Previously, he taught at Chelsea College of Arts, Central Saint Martins, and the School for Poetic Computation, and was Computational Artist in Residence at the V&A.

For enquiries, contact luke@demare.st.
