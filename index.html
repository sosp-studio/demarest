Luke Demarest

Luke Demarest is an artist, researcher, and educator whose practice explores emergence, simulation, and computational pattern-making. He is the founder and director of sosp.studio and teaches at Goldsmiths. Previously, he was a Computational Artist in Residence at the V&A and taught at Chelsea College of Arts, Central Saint Martins, and the School for Poetic Computation.

For enquiries, contact luke@demare.st
